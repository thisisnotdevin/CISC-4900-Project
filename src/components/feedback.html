How should you navigate this website?

With the navigation bar on the top right, you can browse the website. You can start by looking at the trending page to see events that are only happening for a short while. You can also filter them out by category, price, and special events. For more options, you can also choose the main page.

How do I customize my profile?

By selecting the sign in option, you can personalize your profile on our website by entering details about yourself like your name, location, and interests. To save events that you want to attend in the future so that you don't lose them, you should create a profile. Making your profile unique and interesting is ultimately the key to getting others to connect with you and want to learn more about you.

How do I save my preferences?

To save your preferences on a website, you will need to create an account and log in. Once you are logged in, you can access the preferences or settings page, where you can customize various aspects of your account and the website's behavior. For example, you might be able to adjust your privacy settings, choose which notifications you receive, and select your preferred language and time zone. To save your changes, look for a button or link that says "Save" or "Apply" on the preferences page. Clicking this will save your preferences and apply them to your account. Some websites may also automatically save your preferences as you make changes, so you don't have to worry about manually saving them. If you are not logged in or do not have an account, your preferences may not be saved. In this case, you will need to adjust your preferences each time you visit the website.

What is the best way to see the trends?

The most popular way to view trending events is to visit the trending tab. From there, you can use the search bar or drop-down menu to narrow your search to events that are of interest to you, such as those in sports, politics, or entertainment. You can also view the most well-liked events, the events with the most activity recently, or the events that are most pertinent to your interests by using the website's sorting or filtering options. In order to stay informed about the newest trending events as they happen, you can also register for an account and subscribe to alerts or notifications.

How do I search an event that I am interested in?

When you enter the event's name in the search box, the search box will display all relevant results and attempt to match the name in our database.
